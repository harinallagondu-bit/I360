## 🎯 Challenge Overview

> **Scenario:** Monthly Operations Review of 3 store branches — analyzing sales performance, profitability, customer sentiment, and providing data-driven business recommendations.

---

## ✅ Task 1 — Profitability Analysis

**Formula Used:**
```
Net Profit     = Total Sales − Operating Cost
Profit Margin  = (Net Profit / Total Sales) × 100
```

| Branch | Total Sales | Operating Cost | Net Profit | Profit Margin |
|--------|------------|---------------|------------|---------------|
| 🔵 Branch A | $45,000 | $32,000 | $13,000 | 28.9% |
| 🟢 Branch B | $38,500 | $21,000 | **$17,500** | **45.5%** ✅ Best |
| 🔴 Branch C | $52,000 | $48,000 | $4,000 | 7.7% ⚠️ Lowest |

## 🏆 Task 4 — Manager Pitch

### 🎖️ Performance Bonus → Branch B
Branch B delivers the **highest profit margin (45.5%)** with the **lowest operating costs ($21,000)** and the **best customer satisfaction** — making it the clear standout performer. It exemplifies what efficient, customer-focused operations look like and deserves recognition to reinforce this behavior.

### 🔧 Process Improvement Plan → Branch C
Despite generating the **highest revenue ($52,000)**, Branch C's operating costs of **$48,000** leave a dangerously thin margin of just **7.7%**. Combined with negative customer feedback on shipping costs and delivery speed, Branch C urgently needs a structured improvement plan focused on vendor negotiation, logistics optimization, and cost reduction.

---

## ➕ Bonus Challenge — Branch C Cost Reduction

> **What if Branch C reduced Operating Costs by 15% through better vendor negotiation?**

```
Current Operating Cost  =  $48,000
Cost Reduction (15%)    =  $48,000 × 0.85  =  $40,800  (saves $7,200)
New Net Profit          =  $52,000 − $40,800  =  $11,200
New Profit Margin       =  $11,200 / $52,000  =  21.5%
```

| Metric | Before | After 15% Reduction | Change |
|--------|--------|---------------------|--------|
| Operating Cost | $48,000 | $40,800 | ▼ $7,200 |
| Net Profit | $4,000 | $11,200 | ▲ $7,200 |
| Profit Margin | 7.7% | 21.5% | ▲ 13.8 pts |

> 💡 A simple 15% cost reduction would **nearly triple Branch C's profit** — vendor negotiation is the #1 priority action.
